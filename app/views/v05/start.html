The Dart Charge provides vital funds for ongoing transport improvements, including at the Dartford Crossing itself. Around 50 million crossings are successfully paid for each year. Thanks for paying and doing your part.

You must pay for non-UK registered vehicles as well as those from the UK.

Find out about charges, discounts and penalties, including crossings for which there is no charge. How much you pay depends on the type of vehicle and if you already have an account. Sign in to your account if you have one.

Start now

As well as paying for crossings you've made since yesterday, this service allows you to:

  • pay for future crossings automatically by creating an account
  • apply for a local resident discount
  • pay or challenge a penalty charge notice (PCN)
  • check your crossing and payment history

Other ways to pay

The online service accessed above is the quickest way to pay. There are also other options, however. You can:

  • pay with cash at your nearest Payzone store
  • set up an account by post
  • pay in advance or top-up your account by post
  • pay or set up an account by phone by calling the Dart Charge contact centre
  • Phone: 0300 300 0120
  • From outside the UK: +44 (0) 300 300 0120
  • Textphone: 18001 0300 300 0120
  • You can get in touch every day, from 8am to 8pm. Find out about call charges.
